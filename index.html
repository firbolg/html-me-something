Gore Ufgood, Druid

Gore Ufgood is a neutral good druid of the circle of the moon.

His preferred weapons are:

  • A boulder
  • An anvil
  • Bear claws

His skills include:

  • Animal handling
  • Survival
  • Surgery

Some recent accomplishments:

  • Briefly befriending an owlbear
  • Shepherding a fellow druid to his final resting place
  • Partying with his interdimensional friends the Mogu

Top five things he can do:

  1. Call forth a swarm of bees
  2. Turn invisible
  3. Wildshape into all kinds of animals (flight included)
  4. Use his speech of beast and leaf
  5. Create a portable forge by magically heating his metal anvil

Member Druid Council

The druid council is dedicated to preserving the balance of nature through all dimensions for eternity.

It is an equal opportunity bestower, regardless of race, species, or ideals.

The Druid Council reserves the right to treat with appropriate force those who may be under a curse or in the thralls of an evil overlord of any kind.
